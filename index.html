  • The Fender Jazzmaster is an iconic electric guitar model that was first introduced in 1958. Known for its distinctive offset waist body and dual-circuit electronics, the Jazzmaster has become a beloved instrument for musicians across a wide range of genres.
    The Fender Jazzmaster is often associated with surf rock and other styles of music that require a bright, twangy tone. It features two single-coil pickups that are specially designed to capture the full range of the guitar's sound, from rich, warm lows to bright, clear highs.
    One of the most unique features of the Jazzmaster is its dual-circuit electronics, which allow players to switch between two different tone circuits with the flick of a switch. This gives the guitar an incredible range of tonal possibilities, making it a favorite among experimental and adventurous players.
    The Fender Jazzmaster is also known for its comfortable playing experience, with a smooth, fast neck and a well-balanced body that sits comfortably against the player's body. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, the Jazzmaster is a guitar that is sure to inspire you and help you create your own unique sound.

    • We redesigned this guitar’s pickups. They’re packed with the bell-like, articulate tone that made Fender famous—only hotter.

    • Comfortable for chording and single-note playing alike, the 9.5”-radius fingerboard offers modern feel thanks to its medium-jumbo frets, while also making wide bends easier in the upper registers.
